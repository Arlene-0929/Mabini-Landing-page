About Mabini, Batangas

Mabini, Batangas is known for diving and snorkeling in Anilao. The town, named after Apolinario Mabini, has a long history of welcoming visitors. You get a rich marine ecosystem and clear spots for both new snorkelers and experienced divers. The underwater sites offer steady, memorable experiences without feeling crowded or commercial
